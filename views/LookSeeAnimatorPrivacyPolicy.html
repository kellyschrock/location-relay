LookSee Animator Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/02/2017

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.

We use your Personal Information only for providing and improving the Site. By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection And Use

LookSee Animator uses the camera and audio systems on your Android device to take pictures, which are then used to create videos. This constitutes the collection of potentially sensitive data. Nothing is done to distribute the data the application collects, unless you create a video which you subsequently share to a file-sharing site, YouTube, etc. Your privacy is important, so please refrain from using the camera access provided by LookSee Animator to display sensitive information you do not want to share with others.

Log Data

No logs of your activity are recorded in LookSee animator, except for the collections of photos you take which are used to create the aforementioned videos.

Communications

As mentioned previously, LookSee Animator does nothing with personal information and does not communicate the pictures you record to any other party.
