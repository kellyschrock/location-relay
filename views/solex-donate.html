Solex Donation page

Several of the nicest people in the world (Solex users) have suggested I put up a page to take donations for Solex. It's amazing to me that, having paid for an app, someone would want to pay more for it even though whatever additional features I put into it would reach them for free anyway. It's really kind of them to suggest that, but it's also pretty funny.

I guess I can see where these people are coming from. I've used software in the past that I thought was so cool, I wanted to pay for it multiple times. What really gets me is that someone apparently thinks a piece of my software is that cool. I'm grateful for that kind of goodwill. Solex users are the ones who have allowed this kind of thing to continue, and I've really enjoyed working on it!

It feels a bit weird to put up a "give me money" page related to Solex, when you have to pay for Solex to use it anyway. Hopefully this is totally clear: No one, that is NO ONE, should feel obligated in any way to use this page to send me money. But if you do it anyway, I make this pledge: Any money coming to me through this page will be used exclusively for stuff I need in order to improve Solex, or work I do toward the overall goal of replicating (or enhancing) Solo's functionality on the Solo itself, or other birds. Solo is unique among drones in that it's open, allowing people like me (and you!) to build on it and make it do new things. That's what I'm going to do, and I intend to support the efforts of others in the way you're supporting me. The community around Solo is what makes it great.

So, thanks very much for your support! Below is the button.
